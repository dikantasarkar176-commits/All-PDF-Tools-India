Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to PowerPoint conversion?

Our converter preserves most formatting elements including fonts, images, tables, and layout. For complex documents with intricate designs, some manual adjustments might be needed.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to PowerPoint?

Yes! Enable the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) option to extract text from scanned documents and images.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, consider splitting them first or using our desktop application.
