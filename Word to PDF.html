Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Word to PDF conversion?

Our converter preserves most formatting elements including fonts, images, tables, and layout. For complex documents with intricate designs, some manual adjustments might be needed.

Can I convert password-protected Word documents?

No, you'll need to remove the password protection from your Word document before converting it to PDF.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert Word files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, consider compressing them first or using our desktop application.
