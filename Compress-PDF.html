Why Compress Your PDFs?

Smaller files upload and download faster, making sharing more efficient.

Save Storage

Reduce file sizes to save valuable storage space on your devices.

Mobile Friendly

Compressed PDFs load faster on mobile devices with limited bandwidth.

How PDF Compression Works

1

Image Optimization

High-resolution images are downsampled and recompressed to reduce their file size while maintaining visual quality.

2

Font Subsetting

Only the characters actually used in the document are embedded, reducing font data size.

3

Object Compression

PDF objects and streams are recompressed using more efficient algorithms.

Typical Size Reductions

  • Documents with images: 50-70% smaller
  • Scanned documents: 60-80% smaller
  • Text-only documents: 20-40% smaller

Frequently Asked Questions

Will compression reduce the quality of my PDF?

With our intelligent compression algorithms, visual quality is preserved while file size is significantly reduced. For most documents, the difference is barely noticeable.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress PDF files up to 100MB in size. For larger files, consider splitting them first or using our desktop application.

Are my files secure?

Yes! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are never stored on our servers.
