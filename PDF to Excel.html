Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to Excel conversion?

Our converter achieves over 95% accuracy for well-structured tables. For complex layouts, some manual adjustments might be needed.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Excel?

Yes! Enable the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) option to extract text from scanned documents and images.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, consider splitting them first or using our desktop application.
